Frequently Asked Questions

How realistic do the plants look?

Extremely realistic! We use premium silk and PE (polyethylene) leaves shaped from real plant molds. Most visitors genuinely can't tell the difference.

Do they require any maintenance?

Almost none. Just give them a gentle dusting every few weeks with a dry cloth or a soft brush to keep them looking fresh.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied, just send it back for a full refund.

How long do they last?

With minimal care, our faux plants can look gorgeous for 5+ years. They won't wilt, yellow, or need replacing like real plants.

Are they safe for pets?

Yes! Unlike many real houseplants that can be toxic to cats and dogs, our faux plants are completely pet-safe.
